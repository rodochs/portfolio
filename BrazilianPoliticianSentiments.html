BRAZILIAN PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATES - DAILY TWITTER SCORE

The chart below presents, in percentage values (%), the positive tweets about the main candidates for the presidency of the republic in the Brazilian 2022 election. The analysis is performed daily with a sample of 1000 tweets per candidate and takes into account only those tweets with positive or negative content. I.e., tweets that don't show any sentiment are discarded from the analysis.

You can obtain more information about the sentiment analysis methodology used by accessing the project Sentiment Analysis on Pt-br Tweets.
